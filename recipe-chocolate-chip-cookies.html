Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies
cookies
cook time
8-11 min
oven temp
--350°
serving size
3 dozen

Ingredients

  • 2
    cups
    all-purpose flour
  • 1
    tsp
    baking soda
  • 1
    tsp
    salt
  • 1
    cup
    butter, softened
  • 1
    cup
    granulated sugar
  • 1
    cup
    packed brown sugar
  • 2
    tsp
    vanilla extract
  • 2
    eggs
  • 2
    cups
    chocolate chips
  • 2
    cups
    oats

Directions

  1. Combine flou, baking soda and salt in small bowl.
  2. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and canilla is a large mixing bowl until creamy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips and oats.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in preheated 350°F oven for 8-11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
