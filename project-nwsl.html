Game Day

The NWSL websites and apps are designed to give fans what they need on game day as quickly as possible. In progress game are called out and have quick links to live video. Fans who cannot watch the live game stream can follow along with live scores and stats. When they want to dig deeper into the players and teams content directly related to the current match is presented.

News

The experts on the NWSL Media team keep the site and apps flush with content. The news pages are designed to give fans the most up to date articles first and leading them right in to the next article. Those fans looking to dive into background of specific players and team can easily find all relevant articles in one place.

Stats

In depth stats are provided for all players and teams, past and present. The site and apps feature specific player daily so fans can have the opportunity to get a snap shot of the players they may not know much about. These easily digestible stats are balanced out with the detailed stats fans would expect from a professional sports league.

Video

All matches are available for fans to watch live and on demand. The league also provides clips, interviews and other original content for fans. Videos can be found all in one place or on the pages in which the video content is related.

Standings

Researched showed fans wanted to know where their favorite team stands at all points during the season. Standings are given priority on the home page so fans can check quickly as well as a dedicated page which includes more stats.
