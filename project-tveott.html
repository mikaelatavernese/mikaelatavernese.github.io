TV Everywhere OTT Experience

TVOS / ROKU / Samsung TV / Android TV

Download the ROKU App: Lifetime / A&E / History / FYI

Personlized Experience

Due to the use of the remote to navigate set top boxes one click access to what the user wants to watch most is arguably more important on OTT than other platforms. The app is designed to get the user into their desired content as quickly as possible.

Navigate

In finding that users download the app with a specific content in mind the app is designed to simplify the process of locating their desired series.

Decide

In keeping the goal of giving users one click access to the video they’re looking for in mind the app is curated to each user. After they have found the series they want the app presents them with the video they are most likely looking for—whether that be the latest aired or the next video in their binge playlist.

Watch

The app balances giving the user a seamless watching experience while hitting business goals. Where ads cannot be avoided the video player is designed to make all other actions simple. Simple to navigate play/pause buttons, easy scrubbing, video information available on pause and continuous play give users the control they need to simply watch TV on their preferred set top box.
