Personlized Experience

To ease users watching experience the app is designed to put forward what users want to watch most. The app learns which video the individual user wants to watch most and puts it font and center, one tap away.

Navigate

During user research it was found that most users knew exactly what they are looking for when they download to the app. This lead to simplifying the navigation. Putting all of the series on the first screen in alphabetical order with strong visuals allows users an uncomplicated way to find their favorite series. Pairing this with a simple search gives users two ways to get where they are going quickly.

Decide

With constant changes in the broadcast schedule the app has to be flexible enough to handle different amounts of content which may not be available to everyone. The app is designed to guid the user to the exact video they are looking for once the user finds their desired series. The order in which videos have aired and which videos the user has already watched must be clear so the user can quickly find the video they are looking for at that moment.

Watch

The app balances giving the user a seamless watching experience while hitting business goals. Where ads cannot be avoided the video player is designed to make all other actions simple. Easy to tap play/pause buttons, video info available on rotation, continuous play and available Chromecast give users the controls they need to simply watch TV on their mobile devices.
